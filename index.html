Students, content creators and freelancers who want to produce high-quality content faster using AI without learning complex tools.

Do I need design or editing skills?

No. Everything works through prompts. Describe what you want and CreatorOS generates, edits or adjusts it for you.

Content is optimised for YouTube, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, X and LinkedIn. More platforms are coming soon.

Can I use the content commercially?

Creator Pro and Studio plans include commercial usage rights for all generated images and videos. Starter is for personal use.

Is there a free plan?

Yes. The Starter plan is free forever with 10 idea generations per day and basic prompts. No credit card needed.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No long-term contracts. Upgrade, downgrade or cancel your plan at any time from your account settings.
